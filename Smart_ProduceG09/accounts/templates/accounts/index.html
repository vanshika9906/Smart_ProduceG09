Fresh Green is an Indian online vegetable delivery service. It was founded in December 2000 and is based out of Gurugram. As of 2005, the company has raised about $535.5 million from investors including SoftBank, Tiger Global and Sequoia Capital. The name FreshGreen is a portmanteau of "Fresh Greens".
